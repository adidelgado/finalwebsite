About Me

Hello again! As I mentioned on my home page, my name is Adriana Delgado. I prefer to be called Adi. I am 24 years old and a senior in the Strategic Communication program in the Communication and Journalism Department at the University of New Mexico. I am originally from Cuba, New Mexico, but have lived in multiple places throughout the state. I'm the one in the picture above. It would be a bit weird if it wasn't me, right?

I currently work a small hobby farm in Northern New Mexico where I grow a handful of pumpkin varieties, squash, and tomatoes. I enjoy developing recipes that use the crops I grow and plan my personal garden around specific recipes. Along with cooking, I enjoy baking to the point that I officially consider it an amateur hobby. By this time next year, I hope to be able to sell some of my baked goods at craft fairs and other food sales.

As you might have guessed from my blog examples on my portfolio page, writing, and music are two of my favorite hobbies. I enjoy fiction writing and hope to self-publish a novel in the near future. I'm also working on a military sci-fi webcomic with a Europe-based artist and am hoping to have it published in the next few months or so. As for music, I try to keep up with a little bit of everything. I pride myself on the juxtaposition of some of my music tastes, and hobbies in general!
